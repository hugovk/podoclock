Version 1.00 20.09.2010

Plays a random podcast when an alarm goes off.

Podcasts must MP3 files somewhere under E:\podcasts\ 

Keys: 
OK: Play or stop 
Right: Skip 
* or up arrow: Volume up 
# or down arrow: Volume down 
C: Remove alarm, or delete file
